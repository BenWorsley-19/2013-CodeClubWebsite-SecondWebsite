So What Exactly Is Code Club?

We here at Bright Future Software are putting together a new club for 8-18 year olds that will teach you the fun you can have with coding.

We start off with the basics (html and css) then move onto scripting languages such as javascript and php, and eventually we'll even move onto proper programming languages like java and C++. In no time you'll be making your own websites and even creating your own browser based games that you can show off to your friends!
